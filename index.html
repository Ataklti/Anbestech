About Us

Ambes-Tech Trading Plc is a dynamic and forward-thinking company headquartered in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Established with a vision to contribute to sustainable development and technological advancement in the region, Ambes-Tech specializes in providing innovative solutions in the field of solar supply, installation, testing, and commissioning. In addition, the company serves as a technology integrator, addressing diverse societal challenges in Ethiopia by collaborating with overseas partners and leveraging cutting-edge technologies.
